DSU DIFFERENTIATOR

Curriculum

Our curriculum is developed with our philosophy to quality education. Its salient features are that there is coherence in learning, it synthesizes experience and integrates education with experience. The curriculum is designed such that students get constant exposure to industry ready skills and techniques in conjunction with the theoretical fare that is on par with the best in the world.

Board of Studies

Every department is guided by a Board of Studies (BOS), with an eclectic mix of theoreticians and practitioners from the academia and industry. Each BOS is blessed with the brightest of minds from premier institutes like Indian Institute of Science (IISc); Indian Institute of Technology(IIT) and central research institutes. Almost all of our Board of Studies panel is headed by Professors from the Indian Institute of Science.

Teaching Learning Process

We embrace the active learning paradigm for the delivery of our lectures. High quality lecture notes as part of the course pack based on the active learning paradigm have been developed exclusively by our faculty. Courses are delivered using our customization of MOODLE, DOODLE. PIAZZA is integrated into DOODLE to facilitate connection with the faculty beyond the classroom. Learning never stops at DSU.
